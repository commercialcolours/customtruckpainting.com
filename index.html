Professional custom truck painting

When you need a single truck or fleet of trucks painting, you’ll need a professional and reliable company. However, what should you look for in a custom truck painting company?

Specialist custom truck painting company

A specialist custom truck painting service can paint all makes and models of trucks, up to the largest commercial vehicles. They will paint both new and old trucks, and offer services from straightforward panel painting to full body resprays.

From the smallest touch-up to the most extensive paint job, the best custom truck painting services use excellent products and offer every type of paint finish. They use PPG and Glasurit computerised paint mixing systems to match paint manufacturers’ swatches expertly. Together with a dust-free environment, these tools produce a finish which meets even the most stringent standards.

A professional custom truck painting service will also be Office of Fair Trading Approved and EPA registered, have the latest facilities, and skilled technicians with many years of experience.
